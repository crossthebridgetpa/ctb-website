Frequently Asked Questions

Do you build custom AI software?

No. There are great tools that already exist for most business problems. We find the right ones, set them up for your specific workflows, and train your team. No custom code means no maintenance headaches for you.

Can't I just figure this out myself?

You could. Most people who try spend 2-3 months evaluating tools, pick the wrong one, and end up frustrated. We compress that into a week because we've already tested dozens of options. Your time is worth more than the cost of Discovery.

What if AI can't actually help my business?

That's what Discovery is for. If we can't identify at least 5 hours/week of time savings, you don't pay. The guarantee is real.

Will I need to pay monthly subscriptions for the tools?

Most likely yes, the tools we recommend typically have monthly costs ($20-100/mo range depending on the tool). But you'll be paying the tool providers directly, not us. Our job is to make sure you're paying for the right ones and getting full value.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

Professional services, training companies, operations teams, real estate, legal, anyone whose team spends significant time on email, documentation, scheduling, or searching for information. If knowledge work is your bottleneck, we can help.

What happens after the engagement?

You're self-sufficient. We document everything, train your team, and hand over the keys. If you want ongoing support later, we're a phone call away, but you won't need us for day-to-day operations.

How is this different from hiring an IT consultant?

IT consultants manage infrastructure. We focus specifically on AI tools for business workflows: email triage, knowledge management, document generation, scheduling, client communication. It's a niche, and we know it cold.
